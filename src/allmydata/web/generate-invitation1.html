This will be used as your "pet name" for the invitee. Once they accept, you'll see this name on your Control Panel, where you can measure and control how much disk space they're using, and see how much space you're using on their node. This name is made public (so don't use something like "that guy Bob who I pretend to like but really I hate his guts"), but it is specifically for your benefit: the Control Panel will always make it clear when it is displaying one of your Petnames.
